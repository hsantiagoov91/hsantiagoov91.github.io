  • Executive scope. Go to market leadership, warehouse and customer operations, and group level BI systems. De facto GM plus BI director.
  • Employee status. Current control, tools, and core work indicate a W2 relationship. A formal hire reduces misclassification risk.
  • Secure BRM IP. Bring the custom BRM platform in house through employment and IP agreements to protect a mission critical asset.
