Santiago Ortiz

Founder, Vosh Business Solutions

Santiago is a strategic consultant and entrepreneur passionate about helping small and medium businesses grow through clarity, structure, and automation. With over a decade of experience in business development, operations, and digital transformation, he brings a pragmatic, results-driven approach to every project.

Based in Houston, Santiago leads Vosh Business Solutions with a focus on making enterprise-level tools accessible to non-technical business owners — combining tools like AppSheet, Power BI, and AI with a hands-on, personal consulting style.

He believes that simplicity is power, and that technology should support — not overwhelm — entrepreneurs. Vosh is the result of that belief.
