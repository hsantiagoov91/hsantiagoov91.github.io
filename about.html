Who we are

Vosh Business Solutions is a boutique consultancy and systems studio for growth‑minded industrial companies. We specialize in sustainable systematic growth for industrial companies, translating strategy into daily execution: clear processes, practical tools, and measurable outcomes.

Our difference is the BRM approach (Business Results Maximization): a project‑centric way to run sales and operations that unifies quoting, follow‑up, customer experience, and leadership visibility into one operating model.

Focus
  • Industrial supply (OEMs, distributors, EPCs, fabricators)
  • Nearshoring & U.S. expansion playbooks (binational execution)
  • CRM/BRM design, Power BI dashboards, AppSheet/low‑code tools
  • Customer portals, release programs, and OTIF‑driven service

What we do

Nearshoring & GTM

Design practical U.S. routes‑to‑market, distributor/OEM programs, and cross‑border operating standards that reduce lead time and risk.

BRM Systems

How it works

G — Groundwork

Map workflows, tools, and goals. Align on KPIs and customer journey (RFQ → PO → delivery → reorder).

R — Rollout

Stand up your BRM instance, data import, role‑based views, and quick‑win quoting/portal flows.

O — Optimization

Automate follow‑ups, embed dashboards, integrate email/inventory/ERP, and tune the pipeline.

W — Win & Scale

Quarterly reviews, expansion modules (onboarding, quality, shipping), and leadership reporting.
