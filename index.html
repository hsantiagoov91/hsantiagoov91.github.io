Example dashboards & portals

A few of the views we typically install in BRM, Power BI, or your existing tools.

Sales cockpit

One BRM view where managers, reps, and coordinators see performance by manager, region, and contact, plus the open quotes they must follow up today. Less time reconstructing the pipeline from spreadsheets, more time on real conversations.

Customer status portal

A simple portal where selected customers can track orders and releases instantly. They see recent orders, delivery status, and upcoming releases without endless email threads.

Executive scorecard

A leadership scorecard with revenue, margin, backlog, and OTIF on one screen. Perfect for weekly cadences where everyone looks at the same numbers instead of competing spreadsheets.

Customer success overview

An operations dashboard that tracks scheduled orders, in-transit orders, RFQs, and new orders, plus real-time alerts on critical accounts. Gives customer success and operations a shared view of what needs attention.

How we work: The GROW framework

A clear cadence that installs the system without disrupting daily operations.

G — Groundwork

Map workflows, align KPIs, define owners, and set the operating cadence (weekly leadership scorecard, sales huddles, coordinator routines).

R — Rollout

Stand up BRM, import data, configure views, and ship quick-win quotes and portals.

O — Optimization

Automate follow-ups, embed dashboards, integrate systems, and tune pipeline quality.

W — Win & Scale

Weekly KPI cadence, executive scorecard reviews, expansion modules, and leadership reporting.

Weekly KPI cadence

  • Leadership scorecard (revenue, margin, backlog, OTIF) every Monday.
  • Sales huddle dashboard (open RFQs, follow-ups, win rate) twice per week.
  • Coordinator flow board (releases, expediting, critical accounts) daily.
